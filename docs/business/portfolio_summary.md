## LinkedIn or Portfolio Paragraph
I delivered a mine-quarter predictive risk MVP using MSHA operations, incident history, and compliance violations. The project includes a clean data pipeline, conformed views, an 11-feature logistic regression model, and expanding-window backtests across 2019-2021. The current version defers assessed-violation penalties to Phase 2 while still producing actionable risk scores and portfolio-ready BI contract views.

## Demo or Presentation Paragraph
This demo walks through the operational data pipeline, the mine-quarter label construction, and the 11-feature MVP model. It highlights backtest performance across 2019-2021, compares against a naive baseline, and ends with live BI views that surface top-risk mines and KPI summaries. Phase 2 will add assessed-violation penalties and the remaining compliance burden features.
